Grow Closer Through Language

There are many rewarding benefits of learning your partner's or friend's language

  • You grow closer together through understanding their language and culture
  • You connect more deeply to them, their family, and their friends
  • You can create more inside memes and jokes together

Learn a Language Together With Coupling

Coupling makes it easy for your partner to guide you in learning their language

  • Collect flashcards from all over including your conversations
  • Retain every word with the proven spaced repetition flashcard system
  • Ask for help with living comment threads on difficult flashcards
  • Share personal TikTok-style language learning videos
  • Play daily speaking and listening challenges together

About Me

I’m Kevin, a Vietnamese-American. My wife, Heidi, is from China. I’ve been self-learning Cantonese and Mandarin for years using podcasts and Anki.

I wanted to involve Heidi better in my learning process beyond me just asking “how do you say this?”. I started building Coupling for myself and after discovering many couples had similar experiences trying to learn their partner's language.
